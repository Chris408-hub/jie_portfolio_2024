Project Overview:

The Senior University Website is a platform designed to provide learning and recreational activities for elderly individuals. Catering to a diverse range of interests such as piano, dance, musical instruments, philosophy, computer courses, and various physical activities suitable for seniors.

User Research and Needs Analysis:

User research focusing on understanding the specific needs and preferences of the elderly demographic. Factors such as usability, accessibility, and comprehensibility of content were prioritized throughout the design process.

Design Process:

The design process began by incorporating insights from user research, emphasizing user experience design, page layout, and color selection. The chosen color palette of green and yellow was selected based on their association with positivity and vibrancy, tailored to resonate with the target audience of seniors.

Technical Implementation:

The website was developed utilizing a tech stack that ensured user-friendly features for the elderly, including larger font sizes, clear navigation, and an intuitive interface. Emphasis was placed on maintaining stability and security while optimizing accessibility.

Conclusion and Lessons Learned:

Reflecting on this project, valuable insights were gained regarding designing for elderly users. Future recommendations include continual accessibility improvements and the integration of more interactive elements to further engage the senior community.
