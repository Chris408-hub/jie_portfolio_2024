ME ABOUT WORK

Hi, I’m Jie! a front-end developer with a passion for bridging creativity and technology. My professional journey began with a background in international education and interactive media design, and it’s been an incredible ride so far.
I’m a dedicated student, specializing in the captivating realm of Interactive Media Design. My journey has been a dynamic fusion of design, coding, marketing, and media management, but my heart truly beats for front-end development.
I’ve mastered HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to craft captivating web experiences. With a toolbox that includes Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, XD, After Effects and more, I blend aesthetics with functionality to create seamless digital solutions.
I thrive on the intricate dance of web design principles and the art of delivering a flawless user experience (UX). My goal is to not just make websites look amazing but also to ensure they perform at their best.
If you share my enthusiasm for front-end magic or have exciting web projects in mind, I’m always eager to collaborate. Let’s work together to make the web a better place, one line of code at a time!

ME ABOUT LIFE

Hi, I’m Jie! I have a deep love for exploring the world.
